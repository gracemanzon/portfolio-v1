01. introduction

Hello, I am a student software developer and newbie content creator! After a period of self-study I am now enrolled at Actualize Coding Bootcamp and learning full-stack web development. At this time I am working on building a professional portfolio as well as a side-scroller inspired by the 1998 iMac game Nanosaur. I enjoy listening to Harvard's CS50 online lectures, the CodeNewbie podcast and taking time to practice digital painting. Dinosaur Game

A side scroller game utilizing the Phaser 2 framework and inspired by the 1998 iMac game Nanosaur. Written in javascript and featuring key artwork by myself. MVP features player navigation through the platform filled world trying to avoid enemy dinosaurs while collected their eggs to save the species from a 2nd extinction.

InSnapx App

InSnapx is where social media meets instant camera aesthetics. Developed with Ruby on Rails, what originally started as a small social media clone experiment quickly evolved into a portfolio app for retro camera lovers. In the future, users will be able to view each photographer's upcoming events and message them to book or collaborate.
